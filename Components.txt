Arduino Radar System – Components List

1. Arduino UNO
2. Ultrasonic Sensor HC-SR04 (Trig/Echo)
3. Servo Motor (SG90 or MG90S)
4. Jumper Wires
5. Breadboard (optional)
6. USB Cable for Arduino
7. 5V External Supply for Servo (recommended) + common GND
8. Resistors (optional voltage divider if needed for 5V → 3.3V boards)
